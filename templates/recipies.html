Ingredients

Chicken Seasoning

  • 1 3/4 lb (800 g) chicken breast cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tbsp (28 g) cornstarch
  • 1 tsp baking powder

General Tso's Sauce

  • 6 tbsp (90 ml) reduced sodium soy sauce
  • 4 1/2 tbsp (65 ml) hoisin sauce

Method

Prep & Baking

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C), prep 2 baking sheets.
  2. Mix all seasonings in bowl; coat chicken pieces.
  3. Arrange chicken on sheets, spray with oil, and bake for 15-20 mins.

Sauce

  1. Combine all sauce ingredients except cornstarch in saucepan and simmer.
  2. Add cornstarch slurry, cook 2-3 mins until thick.
